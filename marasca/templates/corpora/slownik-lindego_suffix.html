Search can be limited to a specific volume with the within clause, e.g. within vol1,..., within vol6, within vol6part1 or within vol6part2. {% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %}

The within clause can be also used to limit the search to the following sections:

  • body (entries and their corrections),
  • errata (just the corrections),
  • intro (introductory texts),
  • info (other informations),
  • varia (dedications, subscriber lists),
  • front (frontmatter),
  • back (backmatter).
